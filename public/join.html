FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How does the 7-day trial work?▼
You get full access to every feature for 7 days. Your card is required at signup but won't be charged until day 8. Cancel anytime before then — no questions asked, no charge.
What data do you have?▼
We track 1.7M+ licensed real estate agents across the United States, sourced from state regulatory and licensing board databases. Data includes brokerage affiliation, license status, location, and licensing history.
What is Rue?▼
Rue is your AI recruiting assistant. It drafts personalized outreach messages, preps you for objection handling, researches target agents, builds your weekly game plan, and helps you close more recruits. Think of it as a recruiting coordinator who never sleeps.
Can I cancel anytime?▼
Yes. Cancel before day 7 and you'll never be charged. After that, you can cancel anytime from your account settings — no contracts, no penalties.
Is the agent matching confidential?▼
100% confidential. If you're an agent exploring your options, your current brokerage is never contacted or notified. We match you privately based on what matters to you.
